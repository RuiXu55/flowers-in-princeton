### Deptford Pink
![](http://www.astro.princeton.edu/~ruixu/fig/DeptfordPink.jpg)

**Description:** Deptford pink is a species of Dianthus ("pink") native to most of Europe, from Portugal north to southern Scotland and southern Finland, and east to Ukraine and the Caucasus. It is also found in North America. It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant growing to 60 cm tall. The leaves are hairy, dark green, slender, up to 5 cm long.
